# Pinterest for Etsy — TheLittleCoveShop

Pinterest is not a social media platform — it is a visual search engine. People use it to plan purchases, find gift ideas, and discover products months before buying. For a hockey/skating POD Etsy shop, pins drive sales long after posting, especially around the hockey season and gift-giving holidays.

## Core Pinterest Concepts for Etsy POD

### Why Pinterest Works for Hockey/Skating POD

Hockey fans and skating parents are gift planners. They search Pinterest for:
- "hockey gifts for boys", "hockey mom gift ideas", "hockey player gifts"
- "figure skating gifts", "skating birthday party ideas"
- "hockey team gifts bulk", "personalized hockey jersey"

These searches happen 6-10 weeks before purchase. Pins need to be findable weeks before Christmas, playoffs, team banquet season, and graduation.

### Pin Image Specs
- Ratio: 2:3 vertical (1000x1500px ideal)
- Format: JPG or PNG, under 20MB
- Text overlay: 5 words max, legible at thumb size
- Canva tip: Use "Pinterest Pin" template (1000x1500px)

### Pin Types

| Type | Best For | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Standard Pin | Specific listing | Link directly to Etsy listing |
| Idea Pin | Behind-the-scenes, gift guides | No outgoing link — builds awareness |
| Video Pin | Product in use | 4-15 seconds, loop-friendly |
| Carousel Pin | Multiple variants | Swipeable — great for colour options |

## Writing Pin Descriptions

Formula: [Benefit/occasion hook] + [Product with keywords] + [Emotional resonance] + [CTA]

### Hashtag Rules
- Use 2-5 hashtags maximum
- Mix broad (#HockeyGifts) with specific (#HockeyMomLife)
- Put hashtags at the end

### Pin Title
- 40-100 characters
- Lead with main keyword: "Hockey Mom Gift — Personalized Tumbler for the Rink Mom"

## Idea Pin Scripts

Idea Pins get massive reach but don't link out. Use for:
- "5 hockey gifts under $30" (product showcase)
- "How we design a hockey shirt from scratch" (process)
- "Hockey mom survival kit" (lifestyle + product)

### Structure (5 slides)
- Slide 1: Hook — Bold text + eye-catching image
- Slides 2-4: One item or idea per slide — product image + caption + price
- Slide 5: CTA — "Find all of these in our Etsy shop: TheLittleCoveShop"

## Pinterest SEO Keywords — Hockey/Skating Niche

### High-Volume, High-Intent
- hockey gifts for him / her / kids / coach / team
- hockey mom gift ideas, hockey player gift ideas
- personalized hockey gifts, hockey shirt ideas
- skating gifts for girls / boys, figure skating gift ideas
- hockey birthday gifts, hockey stocking stuffers
- hockey christmas gifts, unique hockey gifts, funny hockey gifts

### Long-Tail (Lower competition, higher conversion)
- gifts for hockey goalies, hockey mom tumbler gift
- hockey grandpa gifts, hockey baby shower gift ideas
- peewee hockey gifts, hockey team banquet gift ideas

### Finding More Keywords
1. Pinterest search bar autocomplete = real searches
2. "Related searches" section after a search = gold
3. Pinterest Trends (trends.pinterest.com) for seasonal spikes
