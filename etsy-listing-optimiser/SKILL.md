# Etsy Listing Optimiser — TheLittleCoveShop

Etsy is a search engine first, a marketplace second. Every listing decision — title, tags, description, photos, price — affects how many people find you and whether they buy. This skill applies Etsy-specific SEO logic and buyer psychology to every element of a listing, tuned specifically to the hockey and skating POD niche.

## Before You Begin

Ask the user for (if not already provided):
1. **What is the product?** (Type, design text/theme, variants — size, colour, style)
2. **Which part of the listing needs work?** (Full listing from scratch, or just title/tags/description/pricing?)
3. **Do you have Etsy search data?** (Views, favourites, conversion rate on existing listings — useful for diagnosing problems)

If doing a full listing from scratch, work through each section in order: Title → Tags → Description → Photos → Pricing → Shipping.

## 1. Etsy Title

The title is the most important SEO field on Etsy. It directly determines which searches your listing appears in.

### Rules
- **140 characters maximum** — use all of them
- **Lead with your most important keyword phrase** — Etsy weights the front of the title most heavily
- **No keyword stuffing** — write for both the algorithm and the human reading it
- **Use commas or pipes (|) to separate keyword phrases** — improves readability
- **Include: product type + occasion/recipient + key attribute**

### Formula
```
[Primary keyword phrase], [Secondary keyword phrase] | [Key attribute] — [Brand differentiator]
```

### Hockey/Skating Title Examples

**Good:**
> Hockey Mom Tumbler, Personalized Hockey Gift for Her | 20oz Stainless Steel Insulated Tumbler — Custom Hockey Mom

**Good:**
> Hockey Dad Shirt, Funny Hockey Gifts for Men | Unisex Hoodie — Ice Hockey Fan Apparel, Hockey Player Gift

**Good:**
> Figure Skating Gift, Personalized Skating Tumbler for Girls | Custom Name Ice Skater — Skating Birthday Gift

**Weak (avoid):**
> Cool Hockey Mom Tumbler — Great Gift Idea!
*(Too short, keywords buried, no specificity)*

## 2. Etsy Tags

You have 13 tags, each up to 20 characters (including spaces). Tags are how Etsy matches your listing to shopper searches. Every tag is a search phrase you want to rank for.

### Rules
- **Use all 13 tags — always**
- **Multi-word phrases beat single words** — "hockey mom gift" (3 words) is far better than "hockey" (1 word)
- **No repeating words from your title** in the same form — Etsy already indexes your title; use tags for additional keyword coverage
- **Think like the buyer** — what would they type when searching for this as a gift?
- **Cover multiple occasions** in your tags (Christmas, birthday, Mother's Day, Valentine's, general gift)
- **Mix specificity levels** — one very specific tag, several mid-range, one broader

### Tag Strategy Framework: 4 Types of Tags

| Tag Type | Purpose | Examples |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **Product tags** (3-4) | What the item physically is | "hockey mom tumbler", "hockey hoodie", "skating shirt" |
| **Recipient tags** (3-4) | Who it's for | "hockey mom gift", "hockey dad gift", "gift for skater" |
| **Occasion tags** (2-3) | When they'd buy it | "hockey christmas gift", "hockey birthday gift", "skating party gift" |
| **Attribute tags** (2-3) | Key features | "personalized hockey", "custom hockey gift", "funny hockey shirt" |

## 3. Listing Description

The description serves two purposes: **convincing the buyer** and **signalling relevance to Etsy search** (Etsy indexes description text). Write for the human first — a description that converts is better than one stuffed with keywords.

### Structure

```
PARAGRAPH 1 — The Hook (2-3 sentences)
Lead with the occasion or identity. Make the buyer feel seen. Include your main keyword naturally.

PARAGRAPH 2 — The Product (3-5 sentences)
What it is, what makes it special, the key features. Be specific. Mention materials, dimensions, customisation options.

PARAGRAPH 3 — Who It's For (2-3 sentences)
Paint a picture of the recipient and the moment of giving or using. Emotional resonance.

SECTION — Details (bullet points)
• Material / construction
• Size / dimensions
• Customisation options
• Turnaround time / shipping

SECTION — Shop Info (2-3 sentences)
Brief note about your shop, POD process, quality. Build trust.

SECTION — FAQs (optional but highly recommended)
Answer the 3-4 questions buyers always ask before purchasing.
```

### Description Example — Hockey Mom Tumbler

> **Made for the hockey mom who runs on coffee and team spirit.**
>
> This 20oz stainless steel hockey mom tumbler is the gift she'll reach for at every 6am practice, every tournament weekend, and every game-day celebration. Double-walled vacuum insulation keeps her drink hot for 6 hours and cold for 12 — because rink-side temperatures don't care about your beverage.
>
> Whether you're shopping for a hockey mom, hockey wife, or the woman who basically lives at the arena — this tumbler says exactly what needs saying. Available in 5 colours to match her style (and maybe her team colours too).
>
> **Product details:**
> • 20oz stainless steel, BPA-free
> • Double-wall vacuum insulation (hot 6hrs / cold 12hrs)
> • Leak-proof lid
> • Dishwasher safe
> • Available in: Black, White, Navy, Red, Pink
> • Design printed directly on tumbler — won't peel or fade
>
> **About TheLittleCoveShop:**
> We design and ship all our products with pride. Every item is made to order with care — if you have any questions or want a custom variation, message us and we'll make it happen.
>
> **FAQs:**
> *How long does shipping take?* Orders are typically processed in 2-4 business days and delivered within 5-10 business days.
> *Can I get a custom design?* Yes! Message us with your idea and we'll create something unique.
> *What if it arrives damaged?* We'll replace it or refund — no questions asked.

### Description SEO Tips
- Use your top 3-4 keywords naturally in the first 160 characters (shown in search preview)
- Repeat your primary keyword 2-3 times throughout — not awkwardly, just naturally
- Bullet points are indexed by Etsy — include keywords in them too
- Write "hockey mom gift" rather than "gift for hockey moms" — match how buyers search

## 4. Pricing Strategy

POD pricing on Etsy requires balancing profitability, perceived value, and search visibility (Etsy's algorithm slightly favours listings with strong sales velocity, so pricing too high can slow momentum).

### POD Pricing Formula
```
Base cost (POD supplier) + Etsy fees (~12-15% of sale price) + desired margin = List price
```

### TheLittleCoveShop Pricing Benchmarks

| Product | Budget Range | Sweet Spot | Premium Range |
|---------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| T-shirt | $18-22 | $24-28 | $30-35 |
| Hoodie | $35-40 | $42-48 | $50-60 |
| Tumbler (20oz) | $18-22 | $24-28 | $30-35 |
| Hat | $20-25 | $26-30 | $32-38 |
| Mug | $14-18 | $18-22 | $24-28 |

### Pricing Psychology for Etsy
- **Avoid round numbers** — $27 outperforms $25 and $30 consistently
- **Bundle value** — if you sell a set (tumbler + shirt), price it at 80% of combined individual prices
- **Show sale prices** — original price crossed out with sale price drives urgency (Etsy surfaces sale listings)
- **Free shipping** — listings with free shipping get a slight Etsy search boost; bake shipping into your price

## 5. Photos

Photos are the #1 conversion driver on Etsy. Buyers can't touch the product — your photos do all the selling.

### 10-Photo Strategy for POD Listings

| Photo # | Type | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| 1 (Hero) | Clean mockup — product front | First impression; must stop the scroll |
| 2 | Lifestyle mockup — product in use | Emotional connection; show the recipient using it |
| 3 | Design close-up | Show detail, text clarity, colour accuracy |
| 4 | All colour variants | Helps buyers choose; shows range |
| 5 | Sizing/dimensions graphic | Reduces "how big is this?" messages |
| 6 | Gift scenario | Product as a gift — wrapped, on a gift table |
| 7 | Text overlay — key benefit | "Keeps drinks hot 6hrs" / "Ships in 2-4 days" |
| 8 | Social proof — review quote | Screenshot of a real 5-star review |
| 9 | Flat lay or detail shot | Different angle, different energy |
| 10 | Brand/shop card | Short "about us" visual — builds trust |

### Canva Tips for TheLittleCoveShop
- Use Placeit or Printful mockup templates for lifestyle shots
- Canva's "Brand Kit" ensures consistent colours and fonts across all listing images
- Recommended dimensions: **2700x2025px** (Etsy's preferred ratio is 4:3 for listing images)
- First image should have **no text overlay** — let the product speak first

## 6. Listing Checklist

Before publishing any listing, run through this:

**Title:**
- [ ] Leads with primary keyword
- [ ] 100+ characters used
- [ ] Includes product type + recipient + occasion

**Tags:**
- [ ] All 13 tags used
- [ ] No single-word tags
- [ ] Covers product, recipient, occasion, and attribute types
- [ ] No exact duplication of title phrases

**Description:**
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 160 characters
- [ ] Hook paragraph opens with identity/occasion
- [ ] All product details included (material, size, variants)
- [ ] FAQs section included
- [ ] At least 200 words total

**Photos:**
- [ ] Hero image is clean, bright, product-forward
- [ ] At least 5 photos uploaded
- [ ] Lifestyle shot included
- [ ] All variants shown

**Pricing:**
- [ ] Priced above production cost + Etsy fees + margin
- [ ] Not a round number
- [ ] Free shipping enabled (or baked into price)

## Diagnosing Listing Problems

If an existing listing is underperforming, use this table:

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Low views (< 20/month) | Poor title/tag SEO | Rewrite title to lead with high-volume keyword; refresh all 13 tags |
| High views, low favourites | Weak hero photo | Replace photo 1 with lifestyle mockup; ensure bright, clean background |
| High favourites, low sales | Price too high or trust gap | Check competitor pricing; add review quote photo; add FAQ to description |
| High views, high favourites, few sales | Conversion issue | Check description for clarity; ensure all variants are visible; add urgency |
| Seasonal spike then flatline | Not indexed for evergreen terms | Add non-seasonal tags alongside seasonal ones |
| No traction after 60 days | Listing needs full refresh | Rewrite title + all tags; replace all photos; consider relisting as new |
